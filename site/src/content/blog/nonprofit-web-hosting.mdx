Nonprofits need reliable websites but often operate on tight budgets. Fortunately, many hosting providers offer significant discounts for registered nonprofits.

Here's how to get the best hosting deals for your nonprofit organization.

## Nonprofit Hosting Discounts

### TechSoup Donations

TechSoup is the primary source for nonprofit technology discounts.

**How it works:**
1. Register your nonprofit at techsoup.org
2. Verify 501(c)(3) status (US) or equivalent
3. Access discounted/donated products
4. Small admin fee applies

**Hosting available through TechSoup:**
- Microsoft 365 (heavily discounted)
- Google Workspace for Nonprofits (free)
- Various software donations

## FAQ

### Do I need 501(c)(3) status for discounts?

Most US discounts require 501(c)(3). International nonprofits may qualify with equivalent status documentation.

### Is free hosting reliable enough?

DreamHost's free nonprofit hosting is production-quality shared hosting. For high-traffic sites, consider paid options.

### Should nonprofits use website builders instead?

Wix, Squarespace, and others offer nonprofit discounts too. Consider if you need:
- Custom functionality → WordPress hosting
- Simple site → Website builder may be easier

### How do I prove nonprofit status?

Typically required:
- IRS determination letter (501(c)(3))
- EIN number
- Organization name and address
