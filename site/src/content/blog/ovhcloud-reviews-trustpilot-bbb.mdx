OVHcloud is Europe's largest hosting provider and a go-to for cheap, flat-priced VPS and dedicated servers. Its ratings, though, depend heavily on **where you are** — a 3.8 globally but a brutal 1.2 in the UK. Here's the honest breakdown.

## OVHcloud Ratings at a Glance

| Platform | Rating |
|----------|--------|
| Trustpilot (global) | **3.8 / 5** (~5,700 reviews) |
| Trustpilot (UK) | ~1.2 / 5 |
| Trustpilot (Canada) | ~1.6 / 5 |
| G2 | 4.3 / 5 |
| Better Business Bureau | A- |
| HostDuel editorial | 3.7 / 5 |

The **regional split is the story**. OVHcloud rates well where it has strong local infrastructure and support (much of the EU) and poorly where regional support has been criticized (UK, Canada). The throughline in our scoring: **value 4.5/5 and transparency 4.5/5, but support just 2.5/5**.

## What Reviewers Praise

- **Excellent price-to-resource value** — flat pricing with no renewal hikes (transparency 4.5/5).
- **EU data sovereignty** — owns its infrastructure, a draw for European customers.
- **Wide VPS and dedicated-server range** with consistent pricing.
- **Good for large-scale, self-managed deployments.**

## What Reviewers Complain About

The complaints concentrate on **support (2.5/5)** and a few hard memories:

- **Slow support outside business hours** and a complex interface for newcomers.
- **The 2021 Strasbourg data-center fire** that destroyed some customer data — still cited as a backups cautionary tale.
- **No managed migration, no staging on shared hosting**, and **cPanel/Plesk discontinued for VPS** (bring your own license).
- **Poor regional ratings (UK 1.2, Canada 1.6)** tied to local support experiences.

The honest summary: **OVHcloud is outstanding value with weak support — and your experience depends on your region.** Keep your own backups (the Strasbourg lesson), be ready to self-manage, and it's hard to beat on price.

## Verdict: Who OVHcloud Is For

**Great fit if:** you're a budget-conscious, self-managing developer or business — especially in the EU — who wants flat pricing and data sovereignty.

**Look elsewhere if:** you're in the UK or Canada and rely on local support, you need managed WordPress or staging, or you want 24/7 hand-holding. ## FAQ

### Is OVHcloud legit?

Yes — it's Europe's largest hosting company (founded 1999) with a 4.3 G2 and a BBB A- rating. The low regional Trustpilot scores reflect support experiences, not legitimacy.

### Why is OVHcloud's UK Trustpilot so low?

Regional support quality varies, and UK (1.2) and Canada (1.6) reviewers report slow or unhelpful support. The global 3.8 averages much stronger EU experiences with these weaker ones.

### Is my data safe with OVHcloud after the Strasbourg fire?

The 2021 fire destroyed some customer data and remains a backups cautionary tale. OVHcloud is still widely used, but — as with any host — keep independent off-site backups.

### Is OVHcloud cheaper than Hetzner?

Both are EU value leaders with flat pricing. OVHcloud has a wider product range and more global reach; Hetzner often edges it on raw price-per-resource. Compare both before deciding.
