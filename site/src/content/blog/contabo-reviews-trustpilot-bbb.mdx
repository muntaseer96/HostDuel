Contabo is the name that comes up whenever someone wants maximum VPS specs for minimum money — and also whenever someone complains about support or overselling. Both reputations are earned. Here's what the ratings actually say, plus the tradeoff the stars don't capture.

## Contabo Ratings at a Glance

| Platform | Rating |
|----------|--------|
| Trustpilot | **4.5 / 5** (~9,500 reviews) |
| G2 | 4.0 / 5 |
| Better Business Bureau | N/A (Germany-based) |
| HostDuel editorial | 3.6 / 5 |

The aggregate scores are decent — but they average two very different experiences: people thrilled with the price, and people burned by support or a bad node. Sitejabber and forum threads skew more negative than Trustpilot here, which fits the pattern.

## What Reviewers Praise

Contabo's value rating is a perfect **5.0/5** for a reason:

- **Best specs per dollar** — e.g. ~8GB RAM around $5/mo, with **32TB bandwidth** (the most generous we track).
- **Flat pricing** — no renewal markup.
- **11 global locations**, owns its infrastructure.

## What Reviewers Complain About

The complaints cluster on **support (we rate it 2.5/5) and reliability**:

- **Slow, inconsistent support** — no 24/7 phone; response times vary.
- **Reliability hiccups** — including a widely-reported multi-hour outage.
- **Manual-only backups**, no managed services, no included cPanel.
- **Overselling / performance variability** — the well-known "advertises NVMe, delivers SSD-level speed" complaint. We cover how to detect this in [how to tell if your VPS is overselling](/blog/how-to-tell-if-vps-is-overselling).

## The Numbers Reviews Miss

| Metric | Contabo |

The honest summary: **you trade support and consistency for unbeatable specs-per-dollar.** Fine for non-critical workloads; risky for anything where downtime or slow support hurts.

## Verdict: Who Contabo Is For

**Good fit if:** you want lots of RAM/storage cheaply, you're comfortable on an unmanaged server, and the workload isn't mission-critical (dev boxes, side projects, batch jobs).

**Avoid if:** you need reliable 24/7 support, consistent uptime, or you're a beginner. For more consistent performance and support at a modest premium, [Hetzner](/hosting/hetzner) (best value with better infrastructure rep), [Vultr](/hosting/vultr), or [DigitalOcean](/hosting/digitalocean) — see [best DigitalOcean alternatives](/blog/best-digitalocean-alternatives).

## FAQ

### Is Contabo good or bad?

Both, depending on your needs. It's excellent value (cheapest specs-per-dollar) but weak on support and consistency. Great for non-critical workloads, risky for production sites.

### Why does Contabo have good Trustpilot but bad forum reputation?

Trustpilot captures the many happy price-driven users; forums and Sitejabber amplify the support and overselling complaints. Both experiences are real — it depends which node and support case you get.

### Does Contabo oversell?

It's the most-cited host in overselling discussions, with documented "NVMe but SSD-speed" complaints. Test any VPS for CPU steal and real disk IO — see our [overselling guide](/blog/how-to-tell-if-vps-is-overselling).

### Is Contabo cheaper than Hetzner?

Often yes, for the same specs. But Hetzner has a stronger infrastructure/reliability reputation, so the slightly higher price can be worth it for production.

## Key Takeaways

1. **4.5 Trustpilot** averages two experiences: thrilled-by-price vs burned-by-support.
2. Unbeatable **value (5/5)**; weak **support (2.5/5)** and variable reliability.
3. Known for **overselling** — test CPU steal and disk IO before trusting it.
4. Great for **non-critical, spec-hungry** workloads; wrong for mission-critical sites.
