Vultr is a favorite among developers for cheap, global cloud compute — yet it carries a **2.0 Trustpilot rating**. That looks alarming until you see the 4.3 on G2 and understand where the complaints come from. Here's the honest breakdown.

## Vultr Ratings at a Glance

| Platform | Rating |
|----------|--------|
| Trustpilot | **2.0 / 5** (~520 reviews) |
| G2 | **4.3 / 5** |
| Better Business Bureau | Not rated |
| HostDuel editorial | 3.8 / 5 |

Like other developer clouds, Vultr's ratings split by audience: **G2 developers rate the product 4.3**, while **Trustpilot captures people who hit support or account-verification walls**. The standout in our own scoring is the contrast — **value 4.5/5 and transparency 4.5/5**, but **support just 2.0/5**.

## What Reviewers Praise

- **Excellent price-to-performance** — VPS from as little as $2.50/mo, plus GPU/ML compute.
- **32 global regions** — one of the widest location footprints available.
- **Transparent, pay-as-you-go pricing** — no renewal games (we rate transparency 4.5/5).
- **API-first automation** and crypto payment acceptance.

## What Reviewers Complain About

Every major complaint traces back to **support (we rate it 2.0/5)**:

- **No phone or live chat** — ticket-only, and response times can be slow.
- **Strict account verification** and reports of **account termination without warning**.
- **No money-back guarantee** and **billing only during business hours**.
- **20% surcharge for automatic backups**, snapshots capped at 10, and some load-balancer outage reports.

## The Numbers Reviews Miss

| Metric | Vultr |
|--------|-------|
| VPS from | [$2.50/mo](/hosting/vultr) |
| Global regions | 32 |
| Money-back guarantee | None |
| Value (our rating) | 4.5 / 5 |
| Support (our rating) | 2.0 / 5 |

The honest summary: **you trade hand-holding for unbeatable global compute value.** If you can self-manage and never need to open a ticket, Vultr is superb. If you expect responsive support, the 2.0 Trustpilot is your warning.

## Verdict: Who Vultr Is For

**Great fit if:** you're a developer or startup who wants cheap, global, API-driven cloud compute and you're comfortable being your own sysadmin.

**Look elsewhere if:** you need phone/chat support, a money-back guarantee, or managed WordPress. For 24/7 phone support consider [Linode](/hosting/linode); for managed simplicity see [best DigitalOcean alternatives](/blog/best-digitalocean-alternatives).

## FAQ

### Is Vultr legit?

Yes — it's an established cloud provider (founded 2014) with a 4.3 G2 rating and 32 global regions. The low Trustpilot reflects support and verification complaints, not fraud.

### Why is Vultr's Trustpilot only 2.0?

The complaints cluster on ticket-only support, strict account verification, and occasional account terminations. Developers who self-manage rarely touch support — which is why G2 rates it far higher.

### Does Vultr have a money-back guarantee?

No. There's no refund guarantee, so test with a small deposit before committing real workloads.

### Is Vultr cheaper than DigitalOcean?

Often yes — Vultr's entry compute starts lower ($2.50/mo) and it has more regions, though DigitalOcean has stronger docs and a more polished managed ecosystem.

## Key Takeaways

1. **2.0 Trustpilot vs 4.3 G2** — support complaints vs developers who love the value.
2. Strengths: **value 4.5/5, transparency 4.5/5, 32 regions**, cheap compute.
3. Weakness: **support 2.0/5** — ticket-only, no refund, strict verification.
4. Best for self-managing developers; wrong for anyone who needs responsive support.
