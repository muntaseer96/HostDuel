Kamatera is the cloud VPS people reach for when they want to **configure exactly the server they need** and pay by the hour — one of the most flexible providers around. The ratings are solid, with a few rough edges worth knowing. Here's what reviewers say.

## Kamatera Ratings at a Glance

| Platform | Rating |
|----------|--------|
| Trustpilot | **4.2 / 5** (~320 reviews) |
| G2 | 4.0 / 5 |
| Better Business Bureau | Not rated |
| HostDuel editorial | 4.0 / 5 |

A **4.2 Trustpilot and 4.0 G2** is a consistent, credible profile for a flexible cloud IaaS provider. The standout in our scoring is **value for money (4.5/5)** — granular configuration plus hourly billing means you pay for exactly what you use.

## What Reviewers Praise

- **Granular VPS customization** — dial in CPU, RAM, and storage precisely.
- **Flexible hourly billing** and a genuine **30-day free trial** (rare for cloud).
- **Strong value (4.5/5)** with Intel hardware and good performance.
- **Crypto mining allowed** — unusual, and a draw for specific workloads.

## What Reviewers Complain About

The complaints reflect a developer-first, less-polished platform:

- **No free SSL** — you set up Let's Encrypt manually.
- **Control panel less polished** than competitors, and **documentation could be better**.
- **Limited managed services** unless you pay extra.
- **Crypto mining allowed** can mean noisier network neighbors.

## Verdict: Who Kamatera Is For

**Great fit if:** you're a developer who wants precise resource control, hourly billing, and great value, and you're comfortable self-managing (including SSL).

**Look elsewhere if:** you want managed WordPress, a polished control panel, included free SSL, or a beginner-friendly experience. For more turnkey clouds, see [Vultr](/hosting/vultr) or [best DigitalOcean alternatives](/blog/best-digitalocean-alternatives).

## FAQ

### Is Kamatera legit?

Yes — it's a long-established cloud provider (founded 1995) with a 4.2 Trustpilot and 4.0 G2, known for flexible, configurable VPS instances and a genuine 30-day free trial.

### Does Kamatera include free SSL?

No — you set up SSL yourself via Let's Encrypt. It's straightforward for developers but a hassle if you expected it included.

### Is Kamatera good value?

Yes — we rate value 4.5/5. Granular configuration plus hourly billing means you pay only for the resources you use, which is efficient for variable workloads.

### Is Kamatera beginner-friendly?

Not especially — the control panel is less polished than competitors and documentation is thin. It's aimed at developers comfortable managing their own servers.
