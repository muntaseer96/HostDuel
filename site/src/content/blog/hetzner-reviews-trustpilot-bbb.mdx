Hetzner is unusual: developers rave about it, it tops price-performance comparisons, yet its Trustpilot score is mediocre. That contradiction is the most useful thing about Hetzner's reviews — once you understand it, you know exactly whether Hetzner is for you.

## Hetzner Ratings at a Glance

| Platform | Rating |
|----------|--------|
| Trustpilot | 3.5 / 5 (~2,570 reviews) |
| G2 | **4.5 / 5** |
| Better Business Bureau | N/A (Germany-based) |
| HostDuel editorial | 4.0 / 5 |

This is the widest Trustpilot/G2 gap of any host we track — and it's not noise. **G2's technical reviewers love the value; Trustpilot captures more frustration with support and account verification.** Both are true.

## What Reviewers Praise

Hetzner's strengths are concrete and show in our ratings (value 5.0/5, transparency 5.0/5):

- **Best price-performance in the industry** — VPS from ~$3.49/mo with far more RAM/CPU per dollar than US clouds, and **20TB+ bandwidth** included.
- **Flat pricing** — no renewal markup, ever.
- **Owns its infrastructure** (not a reseller), green energy, German data-protection standards.

## What Reviewers Complain About

Almost every Trustpilot complaint traces to the same root — **support (we rate it 2.5/5)**:

- **Support is business-hours and primarily German**; no 24/7 live chat.
- **Strict account verification** frustrates new users at signup.
- **Account-termination complaints** — the most common serious gripe.
- **No managed WordPress, no cPanel, SSL not free** — it's raw infrastructure.

In other words: the low Trustpilot score is mostly **"I wanted hand-holding and didn't get it,"** not "the servers are bad."

## Verdict: Who Hetzner Is For

**Perfect if:** you're comfortable managing your own server, want the best value in hosting, and don't need hand-holding. The G2 crowd — developers — is exactly the right audience.

**Avoid if:** you need 24/7 live chat, managed WordPress, or beginner support. If that's you, the 3.5 Trustpilot reviews are warning you correctly.

## FAQ

### Why is Hetzner's Trustpilot rating only 3.5 if it's so popular?

Because Trustpilot captures support and verification frustration, while the product itself (value, performance) is excellent — which is why G2's technical reviewers rate it 4.5. The split reflects who's reviewing, not a bad service.

### Is Hetzner reliable?

Yes — it owns its infrastructure and is widely used for production. The weak point is **support responsiveness**, not uptime or hardware.

### Is Hetzner good for beginners?

Not really. There's no managed WordPress, no cPanel, and limited support hours. Beginners are better on a managed host; Hetzner rewards self-sufficient users.

### Does Hetzner have hidden renewal fees?

No — flat pricing with no renewal markup is one of Hetzner's biggest advantages.

## Key Takeaways

1. The **3.5 Trustpilot vs 4.5 G2** split is about **support, not server quality**.
2. Hetzner is the **price-performance leader** with flat, no-markup pricing.
3. The real weakness is **support (business-hours, German-first)** and strict verification.
4. Ideal for **self-sufficient developers**; wrong for beginners wanting hand-holding.
